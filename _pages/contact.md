## Open Positions

Starting this August, a new laboratory will be established at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) within the School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering.

The lab focuses on the following research directions:
- Uncertainty quantification algorithms
- Probabilistic robot perception for grasping and manipulation
- Robust decision-making under uncertainty
- Engineering of robotic systems, including manipulation and field deployment

Our goal is to advance both the theory and practice of uncertainty-aware robotics, with an emphasis on real-world applications and the deployment of reliable robotic systems in complex environments. 

I currently have multiple openings at all levels, including master’s and PhD students, postdoctoral researchers, and research engineers.

Ideal candidates will have:
- Experience in robotics, machine learning, or probabilistic methods 
- Interest in perception, manipulation, or decision-making under uncertainty 
- Hands-on experience with real systems (preferred but not required) 
- Strong programming skills (e.g., Python/C++)
